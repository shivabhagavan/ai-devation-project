ICSR Reporting Timeline Rules

1. Fatal or Life-Threatening cases must be reported within 7 calendar days.
2. Other serious cases must be reported within 15 calendar days.
3. Pregnancy exposure cases:
   - Serious: 11 days
   - Non-serious: 30 days
   - Non-interventional: 60 days
4. Follow-up information for serious cases must be reported within 11 days.
5. Counterfeit or tampering cases must be reported within 11 days.

If reporting exceeds these timelines, it is considered a deviation.
