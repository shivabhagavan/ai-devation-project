Assignable Cause Detail Descriptor (ACDD)

Definition:
A source document or adverse event information was received but was not actioned appropriately.

Examples:
- Information missed in mailbox
- Email sent to wrong person
- Document not attached to correct case
- Translation delay
- Mailbox not checked
- Mis-handled documents

In these situations:
Assignable Cause Category: Process Deviation
Assignable Cause Detail: Case Receipt Error
Assignable Cause Detail Descriptor: Mis-handled Documents
